Yellow

We splashed in cerulean waves-
Caught froth in sandy jars,
Spilled over into each other's arms.
We are dolphins, mermaids, little girls who know
How to make believe.

We offered our bodies to the sea-
Frothy surf and pounds of yellow reasons
To stay.

We bunched the flowers on our dresses into bouquets-
Until,
With a wave,
Our skirts bubbled up
Around our middles
And we, flowers in an obscure ocean
Sank and sank away.
