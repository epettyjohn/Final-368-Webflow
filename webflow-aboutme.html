About Me

I was born and raised in Walla Walla Washington.

From an early age I knew that I would become a writer.

I graduated from Walla Walla High School and am currently attending Eastern Washington University. I am majoring in Technical Communication, minoring in Visual Communication Design, and will graduate with a certificate in Public Relations.

I am currently interning at the YWCA of Spokane. I work in the Women's Opportunity Center. I am working on branding a new program that is being launched in 2014, redesigning materials, and writing a newsletter.

Hobbies

I raise chickens, knit, sew, and make my own cleaning products. I am a member of Eastern Washington University's PRSSA club and am currently knitting winter apparel which I will be donating to local clothing banks to help keep Spokane's homeless population warm this winter.
